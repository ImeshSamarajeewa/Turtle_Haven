Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Preserving and Protecting Sri Lanka's Sea Turtles

Welcome to Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery!

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, situated along the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka, is a remarkable sanctuary for endangered sea turtles. Committed to conservation efforts, the hatchery serves as a vital breeding ground and nurturing center for these magnificent creatures. Skilled caretakers diligently collect and protect turtle eggs, ensuring their safe incubation and subsequent release into the ocean. Visitors can witness the incredible hatching process and learn about the challenges faced by sea turtles. By raising awareness and promoting responsible tourism, the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery plays a crucial role in safeguarding these fascinating creatures for generations to come.

KOSGODA BEACH


Kosgoda Beach, located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka, is a picturesque paradise for beach lovers. With its golden sands, turquoise waters, and swaying palm trees, it offers a serene and idyllic setting. Visitors can relax, soak up the sun, take refreshing dips, or simply enjoy breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. Kosgoda Beach promises a tranquil escape and unforgettable coastal experience.

WILD LIFE EXPLORATION


Embark on a captivating wildlife experience in Kosgoda, Sri Lanka. Immerse yourself in the lush greenery of its wildlife reserves, where you can spot a variety of fascinating creatures like elephants, monkeys, birds, and more. Explore the untamed beauty of nature and create unforgettable memories in this enchanting wildlife haven.
