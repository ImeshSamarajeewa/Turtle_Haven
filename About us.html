About Us

Welcome to our turtle hatchery in Sri Lanka! We are passionate about preserving and protecting sea turtles, and our hatchery plays a vital role in ensuring the survival of these magnificent creatures. Here's some information about us:

Mission:
Our mission is to conserve and rehabilitate sea turtles while raising awareness about the importance of their conservation. We strive to provide a safe and nurturing environment for sea turtles from the time they hatch to their eventual release into the ocean.

Location:
Our hatchery is located in the beautiful coastal region of Sri Lanka, known for its pristine beaches and abundant marine life. This location provides an ideal habitat for sea turtles to nest and for us to carry out our conservation efforts.

Conservation Efforts:
At our hatchery, we actively work towards protecting sea turtles through various initiatives:

  1. Nest Protection: We identify and protect sea turtle nests from poaching and predation by relocating them to a secure hatchery area. This ensures that the eggs have a higher chance of survival.
  2. Hatchery Operations: Our dedicated team carefully monitors the eggs during the incubation period, maintaining optimal conditions such as temperature and humidity. Once the hatchlings emerge, we provide a safe space for them until they are ready for release.
  3. Rehabilitation: We rescue and rehabilitate injured or sick sea turtles found in the surrounding waters. Our team of experts provides medical treatment and care to these turtles, aiming to release them back into the ocean once they have recovered.
  4. Education and Awareness: We believe in the power of education to bring about change. We conduct educational programs, guided tours, and workshops for visitors, schools, and local communities to raise awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation and the threats they face.

Releasing healthy turtles back into the sea is a crucial step in their conservation and survival. After being nurtured and cared for in turtle hatcheries or rehabilitation centers, these turtles are ready to embark on their journey in the ocean. Releasing them allows them to return to their natural habitat and contribute to the balance of marine ecosystems. By ensuring that the turtles are healthy, well-nourished, and strong, we maximize their chances of survival and successful adaptation to their marine environment.

Helping turtles that are trapped inside fishing nets is a critical endeavor for their survival. When entangled, these helpless creatures face the risk of drowning or severe injuries. Prompt action is required to carefully free them, minimizing further harm. Trained professionals or individuals knowledgeable about proper rescue techniques play a vital role in disentangling the turtles, ensuring their safety and well-being. By providing assistance in such situations, we contribute to reducing the negative impact of human activities on these magnificent marine animals and promote their conservation.

Caring for turtles in a turtle hatchery involves dedicated efforts to ensure their well-being. The hatchlings require a controlled environment with optimal temperature and humidity levels for their healthy development. Feeding them a nutritious diet and providing a safe space for growth and protection are crucial. Regular monitoring, veterinary care, and expert guidance aid in their overall health. By nurturing these young turtles, we strive to enhance their chances of survival and contribute to the conservation of these remarkable creatures.
