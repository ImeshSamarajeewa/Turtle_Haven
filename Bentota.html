Bentota Turtle Hatchery

Preserving and Protecting Sri Lanka's Sea Turtles

Welcome to Bentota Turtle Hatchery!

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery, located in the charming coastal town of Bentota, is a haven for endangered sea turtles. Dedicated to conservation and protection, the hatchery plays a vital role in nurturing and releasing thousands of hatchlings into the wild each year. With a passionate team of experts, they provide a safe environment for eggs to hatch, safeguarding the vulnerable species from predators and human interference. Visitors can witness the magical sight of tiny turtles making their way to the sea, fostering awareness and appreciation for these magnificent creatures. The Bentota Turtle Hatchery stands as a beacon of hope for the survival of sea turtles.

BENTOTA BEACH


Bentota Beach, nestled on Sri Lanka's southwestern coast, captivates visitors with its pristine sands, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil atmosphere. The beach offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, inviting guests to unwind, swim, indulge in water sports, or simply bask in the sun's warm embrace.
